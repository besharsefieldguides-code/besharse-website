Our Story & Beliefs

Based in Monette, Arkansas. Built for the natural world.

How We Started

Besharse Field Guides started from a lifelong love for nature. We are based in Monette, Arkansas, surrounded by a wide variety of wildlife. Growing up here taught me that to truly understand animals, you have to spend time with them, respect them, and look at the facts instead of believing old myths.

Our main goal is simple: education is the best way to protect wildlife. The more people know about the animals around them, the less they will fear them. What started as my own personal hobby has grown into a big mission to find, study, and protect animals all over the world.

How We Make Our Guides

A field guide needs to be completely accurate. At Besharse Field Guides, we do the hard work outside in nature to make sure every detail is right. We spend hundreds of hours tracking and watching animals in their natural homes. We don't just read books to get our information; we go out and see it for ourselves to make sure it is true.

We keep our standards high because our readers trust us. Whether you are a scientist or just someone who loves the outdoors, you can depend on our guides. If you are trying to identify a snake by its scales or figure out what kind of bird is in your yard, you can know our facts have been checked and proven.

Giving Back to the Community

Our work doesn't stop with our books. We spend time teaching our local community about nature. We believe that if we show people how to safely act around wildlife, they will want to help protect these animals for the future.

We work with local groups, host learning events, and give schools the materials they need to teach kids about the outdoors. My biggest dream is to build a large wildlife center where we can rescue animals and teach the public, reaching far beyond our home in Arkansas.
