Our Mission & Vision

Educating, conserving, and uncovering the truth about the wildlife created to share our world.

Why Besharse Field Guides?

At Besharse Field Guides, we provide the most comprehensive, expert-level information about wildlife. From identifying a snake or spider to a bird, we offer detailed insights—including range maps and behavioral profiles—that go far beyond basic identification.

My journey began when I was just 10 years old. Over the last decade, I have dedicated my life to the study, documentation, and conservation of wildlife, with a primary focus on the species often misunderstood by society: snakes and spiders. I believe that every creature has a purpose and a place in creation, designed intentionally by God. My goal is to use this work to dispel the myths, irrational fears, and misinformation that have persisted for generations.

Addressing Myths and Conservation

Too often, fear of snakes and spiders stems from a lack of understanding of their history and their true nature. My work delves into the origins of these fears, uncovering the misconceptions that have prevented people from appreciating these animals. Many of these species are vital to our environment, helping to control pests and maintain balance in the ecosystems they inhabit.

Through these field guides, I strive to show that these animals are not here to harm or cause fear, but to fulfill their unique roles in the natural order. Whether I am researching the historical significance of a species or documenting a specific specimen in the wild, my experience continues to grow as I refine our guides. Thank you for joining me on this mission to conserve wildlife and educate the public on the beauty of God’s creation.
