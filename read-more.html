The Vision:
A Global Sanctuary

Building the world’s largest wildlife conservation center.

What began as a dream in Ozark, Arkansas, has evolved into a global mission. We are not just protecting our local ecosystem—we are building a legacy to become the largest wildlife conservation center in history. From the heart of the Ozarks to every corner of the globe, our reach will ensure no species is left without a protector.

Worldwide Protection

Our initiatives extend beyond borders, providing resources, medical expertise, and sanctuary space for wildlife across every continent.

Unrivaled Education

By building the largest facility of its kind, we will host researchers and educators to create a global hub for wildlife knowledge and public awareness.

Our 100% Transparency Guarantee

I take zero profit. Every single dollar donated is funneled directly into the foundation to build, staff, and maintain our global conservation facilities.

This isn't just a project; it's a necessity. With your support, the Ozark-based center will be the headquarters for a movement that saves lives, restores habitats, and educates billions. Together, we are building a future where wildlife thrives.
