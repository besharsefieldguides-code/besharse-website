Building the Future of Wildlife

My big dream is to establish the largest wildlife conservation center ever built. While our roots are being planted in Ozark, Arkansas, our vision is global. We are dedicated to saving wildlife and educating the public worldwide.

This facility will be a sanctuary where animals are cared for, rehabilitated, and protected. Education is at the core of what we do, ensuring that everyone understands the importance of our natural world.

100% Transparency:

I do not make a profit from your donations. Every single cent goes directly to the foundation for the facility and the care of the animals. Your support builds the sanctuary, provides medical care, and saves lives.
