The Dream: Ozark Wildlife Center

Building a sanctuary in Arkansas.

My big dream is rooted in the heart of Ozark, Arkansas. I am dedicated to building a state-of-the-art wildlife center focused on the rescue, rehabilitation, and long-term care of our local fauna. Nature is a delicate balance, and this facility will serve as a sanctuary for animals in need and a beacon of hope for conservation.

Education & Preservation

Beyond medical care, our mission is to educate the public. By fostering a deep connection between the community and the natural world, we can inspire the next generation of environmental stewards right here in Arkansas.

🌿 100% of your donations go directly to the facility foundation.

I do not take a profit from these donations. Every cent contributed is channeled directly into construction, medical supplies, and the daily operations of the foundation. Together, we can ensure that the wildlife of the Ozarks has a fighting chance.
