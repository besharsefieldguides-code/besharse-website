The Dream: A Global Sanctuary

Building the world's largest wildlife center, starting in Ozark, Arkansas.

My big dream is to establish the largest wildlife conservation center ever built. While our headquarters are in Ozark, Arkansas, our reach is global. I am dedicated to building a state-of-the-art sanctuary for the rescue, rehabilitation, and long-term care of wildlife worldwide.

Education & Preservation

Beyond medical care, our mission is to educate the public across the globe. By fostering a deep connection between communities and the natural world, we can inspire a new era of conservationists.

🌿 100% of your donations go directly to the facility foundation.

I do not take a profit from these donations. Every cent contributed is channeled directly into construction, medical supplies, and the daily operations of the foundation facility. Together, we are building a future where wildlife thrives.
