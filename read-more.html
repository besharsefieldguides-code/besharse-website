The Global Mission

My dream is to build the largest wildlife conservation center ever. Based in Ozark, Arkansas, our facility will serve as a global hub for saving wildlife and educating the public.

I am dedicated to ensuring that every animal has a voice and every person has the knowledge to protect them.

100% of your donations go directly to the foundation facility. I take 0% profit. Every cent goes toward the care and protection of wildlife.
