Our mission is to establish a premier Wildlife Rescue and Education Center in Ozark, Arkansas, rescuing global species and educating communities through comprehensive field guides.

Our Vision

The Ozark, Arkansas Wildlife Center Project

We are working toward a big goal: building a state-of-the-art wildlife center nestled in Ozark, Arkansas. This facility will serve dual noble purposes: rescuing injured and displaced animals from all corners of the globe, and maintaining specialized educational species groups to teach the public firsthand about conservation engineering, biological systems, and habitat health.
